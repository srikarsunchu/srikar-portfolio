▶ Background


Nerv Studios is a concept branding project. I built it to explore systematic identity work—the kind that's built on grids and principles rather than trends.


The whole identity follows Swiss design thinking. Strict layouts, minimal color, typography doing the heavy lifting. I wanted to see how restrained I could get before it started feeling sterile. Turns out, pretty far.


It's functional branding. No flourish, no decoration—just clear visual systems that work across every touchpoint.

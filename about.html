/ Readme.md

I'm Srikar—design engineer, co-founder at 9M Holdings, and recovering caffeine addict based in San Francisco. I studied Graphic Design at San Jose State, interned in UX Research at Adobe, and somewhere along the way fell in love with building interfaces that don't suck.

When I'm not pushing pixels or debugging React, you'll find me making short films, hunting down obscure Japanese jazz records, or obsessing over typography that nobody asked me to fix. I believe great design is invisible and great code is honest. Everything here was built with intention, craft, and probably too much espresso.

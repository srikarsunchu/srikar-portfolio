/ Readme.md

Srikar is a design engineer and filmmaker based in San Francisco. He is the Design/Media lead at Elide, where he builds the visual and narrative layer around a next-generation polyglot runtime.

Previously he was a design engineer at 24labs, helping build and scale bootstrapped SaaS products to $200K MRR. He's also contracted with design agencies on interfaces and interactive web experiences, and produced films and edited video for YouTubers and streamers.

Somewhere along the way he fell in love with building interfaces that feel as good as they function. He hasn't stopped since.

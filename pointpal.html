▶ Overview


Built for 24labs and launched with Graham Stephan. PointPal is an AI-powered travel platform that helps users find flights 40-90% cheaper by optimizing credit card point transfers. The platform searches across 200+ airlines in real-time to identify the best redemption opportunities, often providing 2-5x better value than traditional cash back or travel portals.


As design engineer, I built the landing page and developed the application—from the smart search interface that queries 100,000+ flights to the personal AI assistant that guides users through complex point transfers. The platform integrates with 5+ major card programs (Chase Sapphire, Amex Gold, Capital One Venture X) and provides live deal alerts to help users maximize their points. Over 40,000 flights have been booked through the platform.


The challenge was making travel hacking accessible—translating complex point transfers and airline partnerships into a simple, intuitive interface. Users can now find premium cabin flights for a fraction of the points, with clear instructions on exactly how to book them.
